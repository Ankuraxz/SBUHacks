What is SBUHacks?

At SBUHacks, you can deviate off the beaten-path and work on a completely new, fresh and exciting project. Whether it's based on your passions or a completely new topic, spending time on projects outside the classroom is the best way to not only learn what you love, but find new interests. In a universe of infinite possibilities, you have 24 hours to go far beyond and show us what you got!
